The History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the mission of bringing together innovators, leaders, and creators from the tech industry to share knowledge, discuss emerging technologies, and shape the future of tech. Over the years, the conference has grown into one of the leading events in the technology sector.

The first TechCon conference took place in 2010 in San Francisco and was attended by over 500 participants. Since then, TechCon has expanded globally and hosted thousands of tech enthusiasts, professionals, and companies worldwide.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to inspire, educate, and connect the global tech community. Our goal is to provide a platform for sharing innovative ideas, discussing the latest trends, and collaborating on projects that drive positive change in the tech world.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in the tech world. Our past speakers have included industry pioneers, thought leaders, and innovators who have shaped the way we interact with technology.

Elon Musk

Elon Musk, CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, shared his vision for the future of space exploration and sustainable energy at TechCon 2020. His talk focused on the advancements in electric vehicles and the pursuit of multi-planetary life.

Satya Nadella

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, discussed the impact of cloud computing on modern enterprises and how artificial intelligence is transforming industries at TechCon 2019.

Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, COO of Facebook, spoke at TechCon 2018 on leadership in the tech industry and the importance of diversity in tech teams. Her insights on corporate culture and innovation were inspiring for many attendees.
